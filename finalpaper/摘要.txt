ICTV在2016年的第10次会议中对病毒的分类做出了巨大的调整，这使得过去的分类系统不再适用于新形势下的病毒发育谱系学研究。同时，高通量测序得到的大量病毒基因组数据和越来越丰富的蛋白质结构-功能数据使得从病毒基因组中获取病毒蛋白质序列成为可能。虽然不同种的ssRNA病毒在形态，结构以及繁殖方式上存在巨大的差异，但最近的研究结果表明这些病毒可能具有相同的进化起源，为了确定不同种病毒之间的发育关系，我们使用邻接法来构建可能的系统进化树。我们的研究强调了含有相同RdRp的病毒具有亲缘性，我们的研究表明，除了RdRp族内的RdRp，其余的RdRp均可以作为确定病毒所属目的证据。最近的证据表明，基于在某些RdRp序列构建的进化树上发现的具有相似性的病毒在分类学上也具有相似性，但是含有其他RdRp的病毒应该在以后的研究中继续调查。总之，我们的结果为病毒的分类提供了新的思路，从而为病毒在目一级的分类学，病毒的多样性分析和基因组演化等基本问题提供了新的视角。
ICTV made a huge adjustment to the virus classification at the 10th meeting in 2016, which made the past classification system no longer applicable to the developmental pedigree studies under the new situation. At the same time, a large amount of viral genome data obtained through high-throughput sequencing and increasingly rich protein structure-function data make it possible to obtain viral protein sequences from the viral genome. Although there are huge differences in morphological, structural and reproductive patterns among different species of ssRNA viruses, recent studies have shown that these viruses may have the same evolutionary origin. In order to determine the developmental relationship between different species of viruses, we use the Nighbor-Joining method to build a possible phylogenetic tree. Our study emphasizes that the viruses containing the same RdRp are related, and our study shows that, in addition to RdRp within the RdRp family, the remaining RdRp can be used as evidence for the purpose of determining the virus. Recent evidence suggests that viruses based on similarities found in phylogenetic trees constructed with certain RdRp sequences are similar in taxonomy, but viruses containing other RdRp should be investigated in future studies. In summary, our study provide new insights into the classification of viruses, providing a new perspective for virus differentiation at Order level, virus diversity analysis, and genome evolution.
